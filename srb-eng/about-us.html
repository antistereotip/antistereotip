A few words about us ?

Foundation "RAJAC - a small paradise on Earth"

The Foundation is established for the implementation of the Project which initiates and accelerates the economic development of the Municipality of Ljig, subprojects on the mountain Rajac and the villages Slavkovica and Veliševac, on the basis of a joint private initiative, with the use of all available measures of support of the Republic of Serbia and local self-government. After creation material assumptions, the Foundation will in the coming period intensify work on the development of all types of d activities (culture, art, tradition, sports, various forms of humanitarian activities, music, educational activities, publishing, as well as cooperation with the media, etc.)

MEMBERS

Board of Directors: Vera Josipović, President; Dr. Nikola Zelic; Petar Dopuđ

Director of the Foundation: Milan Josipović
