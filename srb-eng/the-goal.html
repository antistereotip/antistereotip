Objectives stated in detail ?

1. Contribution to a more significant increase in employment of the local population and GDP, which would have an impact on employment and GDP of the Republic as a whole.
2. Reaching the level of development and diversity of the economic structure, which will facilitate inclusion Ljig and Rajac in the sphere of interest of the city of Belgrade and other centers, which will be further encouraged, at the end of the section of the Belgrade-Ljig highway on Corridor 11.
3. Export of technology to the world - sale of domestic knowledge, technology and personnel to the world (certain types products are already ready for export)
4. Providing humanitarian aid and eliminating the consequences of natural disasters (especially flood-regulation of river flows). The Foundation will especially support the humanitarian activities of the Church.
5. Nature conservation and protection of the human environment (with ethno approach in architecture)
6. Supporting the development of infrastructure facilities in the Municipality of Ljig
7. Awarding scholarships to talented individuals from the territory of the Municipality of Ljig and the whole of Serbia
8. Cooperation with neighboring municipalities, primarily Gornji Milanovac and Mionica (especially in relation to the cave on Rajac and Banja Vrujci) and the city of Belgrade (primarily in the field of tourism on the route Belgrade-Ljig-Rajac), as well as with the municipality of Sremska Mitrovica in connection with the research project and excavations of the Roman "Sirmium". Cooperation with the Republic of Montenegro regarding the idea of ​​creating a "Pero-town" in Kruševice above Bijela, all with the aim of enriching the tourist offer and increasing the attractiveness of Serbia and the Bay of Kotor for foreign countries and domestic tourists.
9. Promotion of Rajac, Ljig and Serbia in the world (through a travel agency that will be established at Foundation) and major sporting events in Serbia and the world, in which athletes play a significant role Of Serbia.

10. Through its activities and the operations of companies established under the auspices of the Foundation, the Foundation will force application of the principle "Let's buy domestic and sell domestic!"
11. Returning to nature and a healthier way of life (through various types of tourism and training of people, especially youth for a healthy diet, which will reduce the cost of health care for the population. Among other things, publications and magazines that contribute to a healthy diet will be published population and adequate supply in catering and tourism).
12. Development of sports infrastructure with the aim of creating all the necessary preconditions for dealing with youth and older categories of the population by sports in areas where top results already exist (primarily volleyball, football and chess), but also in other areas of sport for which there is a pronounced interest (tennis, badminton, etc.), as well as recreational activities of the entire local population sports activities.
13. That people are gradually returning from the city to the village, that abandoned houses are used as efficiently as possible facilities, as well as land that is no longer used or cultivated. This will be best achieved in an accelerated manner by developing the economic structure of the Region and increasing employment, not only from local, but also from external sources.
14. Cooperation with other foundations and institutions from the country and abroad that deal with respectively activities.
15. Within the framework of cooperation with other countries, special attention will be paid to the development of exchange of goods, services and staff from all countries of the former Yugoslavia, as well as Greece, Russia, Italy, Norway, Austria, Cuba and China, etc. starting from traditional friendly relations and cooperation between Serbia and the mentioned countries, but also from the friendly, family, business and personal ties of the founders Foundations with individuals and / or companies and institutions from these countries.
16. Analytical monitoring of all global statistical indicators of the Municipality of Ljig, by places on an annual basis level with the aim of reviewing the contribution of this foundation to the economic and overall social progress of the Region.
